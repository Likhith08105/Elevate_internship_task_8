INSIGHTS FROM SUPERSTORE SALES DASHBOARD

1️⃣ The West region recorded the highest overall sales, contributing approximately one-third of the total revenue, while the South region showed the lowest sales volume.

2️⃣ Among all product categories, the Technology category generated the maximum sales share, followed by Office Supplies and Furniture.

3️⃣ The monthly trend indicates a steady increase in sales throughout the year, with noticeable peaks during Q3 and Q4 — suggesting seasonal demand.

4️⃣ The cumulative sales line shows a consistent upward trajectory, confirming continuous business growth and no major sales decline periods.

5️⃣ Average sales per order remained stable across all regions, reflecting balanced performance and effective pricing strategy.
